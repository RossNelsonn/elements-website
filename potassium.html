POTASSIUM

Potassium is known as an alkali metal which is soft and silvery white in colour. When it burns, it produces a purple coloured flame.

Where is Potassium found?

Potassium is not available in nature as a pure element. It is found in various minerals such as sylvite and kainite. Most minerals that contain potassium are referred to as potash.

How is Potassium used today?

Potassium is used in the making of soaps, detergents, gold mining, glass production, gunpowder,fireworks and batteries.

Interesting Facts about Potassium

Some good sources of potassium are in food, such as bananas, nuts, chocolate, parsley and potatoes.
